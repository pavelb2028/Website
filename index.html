ABOUT EL JEFE'S
SOBRE EL JEFE'S

El Jefe's Tire Shop is Fort Worth's trusted neighborhood tire and auto shop, located right on E Rosedale St. We've built our reputation serving our community with honest work and fair prices.

We know how important your vehicle is to your daily life — that's why we treat every car like it's our own. From a quick flat fix to a full brake job, we get it done right the first time.

We're proud to serve both English and Spanish-speaking customers with the same level of care and professionalism.

El Jefe's Tire Shop es la llantería y taller de confianza del vecindario en Fort Worth, ubicados en E Rosedale St.

Sabemos lo importante que es tu vehículo — por eso tratamos cada carro como si fuera el nuestro.

Atendemos con orgullo a clientes de habla inglesa y española con el mismo nivel de cuidado y profesionalismo.
